UX/UI DESIGN

UX INTERVIEW

задача

Many people want to reduce social networks use, but they are afraid no longer being notified about local events happening around them. However, searching for technology events often requires them to use several different online sources, what is often time consuming and laborious for many people.

рішення

Tech.co is a web application designed with a user centered approach. Event Listings are customised around the user’s location, topics, format, entry fee and rating. Furthermore the user does not have to navigate through numerous technology events before they find events happening around them. As a result user can safe his time.
